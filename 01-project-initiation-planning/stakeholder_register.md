## Stakeholder Roles and Responsibilities

### Internal Stakeholders

#### Production Team
**Production Manager (SH-001)**
- **Role:** Primary business owner and decision maker
- **Responsibilities:** Define business requirements, approve solution design, validate functionality
- **Expectations:** System meets production needs, minimal disruption during implementation
- **Engagement Level:** Daily during requirements, weekly during development

**Production Supervisor (SH-002)**
- **Role:** Process expert and operational lead
- **Responsibilities:** Provide detailed process knowledge, participate in testing, train operators
- **Expectations:** Easy-to-use system, improved efficiency
- **Engagement Level:** Weekly during requirements, as-needed during development

**Production Operators (SH-003)**
- **Role:** End users of the system
- **Responsibilities:** Participate in UAT, provide feedback, adopt new system
- **Expectations:** Simple interface, clear instructions
- **Engagement Level:** UAT phase, training sessions

#### IT Department
**IT Project Manager (SH-004)**
- **Role:** Project leadership and delivery oversight
- **Responsibilities:** Manage project timeline, resources, budget, risks
- **Expectations:** Successful delivery within constraints
- **Engagement Level:** Daily throughout project

**Kubal System Administrator (SH-005)**
- **Role:** Technical system owner
- **Responsibilities:** System architecture, integration design, deployment
- **Expectations:** Maintainable, secure solution
- **Engagement Level:** Daily during development and deployment

**System Developers (SH-006)**
- **Role:** Implementation team
- **Responsibilities:** Develop solution per specifications, unit testing, bug fixes
- **Expectations:** Clear requirements, access to test environments
- **Engagement Level:** Daily during development phase

**Business Analyst (SH-007)**
- **Role:** Requirements gathering and documentation
- **Responsibilities:** Elicit, document, validate requirements, bridge business-IT gap
- **Expectations:** Stakeholder cooperation, timely feedback
- **Engagement Level:** Daily during requirements, regular during development

#### Quality Department
**Quality Assurance Manager (SH-008)**
- **Role:** Quality oversight and compliance
- **Responsibilities:** Ensure print accuracy meets standards, validate customer requirements
- **Expectations:** System maintains quality standards
- **Engagement Level:** Weekly during requirements, during UAT

**QA Inspectors (SH-009)**
- **Role:** Quality verification users
- **Responsibilities:** Verify laser print output meets specifications
- **Expectations:** Accurate output, easy verification
- **Engagement Level:** UAT phase

#### Customer Service
**Customer Service Manager (SH-010)**
- **Role:** Customer requirements liaison
- **Responsibilities:** Provide customer-specific requirements, validate layouts meet customer needs
- **Expectations:** System supports diverse customer needs
- **Engagement Level:** Weekly during requirements gathering

#### Management
**Operations Director (SH-014)**
- **Role:** Executive sponsor
- **Responsibilities:** Provide strategic direction, approve budget, remove blockers
- **Expectations:** ROI achievement, risk mitigation
- **Engagement Level:** Monthly status updates, critical decision points

**IT Director (SH-015)**
- **Role:** IT sponsor
- **Responsibilities:** Approve technical approach, allocate IT resources
- **Expectations:** Aligned with IT strategy and standards
- **Engagement Level:** Monthly status updates, technical decision points

### External Stakeholders

#### Hertwich Company
**Hertwich Technical Contact (SH-012)**
- **Role:** Integration partner - technical expert
- **Responsibilities:** Provide API/integration specifications, support integration testing
- **Expectations:** Clear data requirements, advance notice of changes
- **Engagement Level:** As-needed during integration design and testing

**Hertwich Project Manager (SH-013)**
- **Role:** External partner coordination
- **Responsibilities:** Coordinate Hertwich resources, approve integration approach
- **Expectations:** Minimal disruption to current operations
- **Engagement Level:** Bi-weekly during integration phases
