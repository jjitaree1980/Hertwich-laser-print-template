## Workshop Best Practices

### Before the Workshop
- [ ] Send invitations 1-2 weeks in advance
- [ ] Send agenda and pre-work 3-5 days before
- [ ] Confirm attendance 2 days before
- [ ] Book room and arrange equipment
- [ ] Prepare materials (flip charts, markers, sticky notes)
- [ ] Set up collaboration tools (if virtual)

### During the Workshop
- [ ] Start and end on time
- [ ] Review objectives and agenda at start
- [ ] Use parking lot for off-topic items
- [ ] Encourage participation from all attendees
- [ ] Capture outputs visually
- [ ] Take photos of flip charts/whiteboards
- [ ] Assign clear action items with owners
- [ ] Summarize key outcomes at end

### After the Workshop
- [ ] Send thank you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Distribute meeting minutes within 48 hours
- [ ] Share photos of workshop outputs
- [ ] Follow up on action items
- [ ] Schedule follow-up sessions if needed
